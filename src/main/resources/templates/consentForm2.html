Dear Participant,

My name is Scott Schanke and I am a faculty member in the Management Information Systems Department at the University of Georgia. I am inviting you to take part in a research study.

Purpose:

In this research we are interested in how individuals perceive recommendations for music that use different forms of user data. We are interested in how users respond to these recommendations.

You are being included in this project because you are living in the United States, and at least 18 years of age.

If you agree to take part in this research, you will be asked to record a brief interview of yourself answering a spontaneous question, then you will answer a few questions in a survey about your background. We will then recommend some music samples for your evaluation. This participation will occur online through Prolific.

Participation is voluntary. You can refuse to take part or stop at any time without penalty.

Risk:

The potential risk of this study is that subjects may feel some light discomfort recording their data, or may not like the song that is recommended.

Benefits:

There is no direct benefit to you for this research. Your responses may help us understand how people respond to algorithmic recommendations made using various types of input data. This can help to form privacy policy, and best practices.

Research records will be labeled with study IDs via prolific, and we will not be able to identify you within the data. You will be paid via the Prolific platform.

Data collected in this research will be used solely for this study.

Incomplete Disclosure:

In order to make this study a valid one, some information about the purpose of the research and the recommendation process will be withheld until the completion of the study. At the end of the study you will be provided with additional information explaining the full purpose of the research.

If you are interested in participating or have questions about this research, please feel free to contact me at 920-284-7258, scott.schanke@uga.edu. If you have any complaints or questions about your rights as a research volunteer, contact the IRB at 706-542-3199 or by email at IRB@uga.edu.

Please keep this letter for your records.

Scott Schanke
