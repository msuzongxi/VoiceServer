We’re inviting you to participate in a research study. Participation is completely voluntary. If you agree to participate now, you can always change your mind later. There are no negative consequences, whatever you decide.


What is the purpose of this study?

The purpose of this study is to explore how voice data can provide further information for the recommendation of a person's music preferences.


What will I do?

The procedure proceeds as follows.

If you agree to be in this study, you will be asked to verbally answer 1 icebreaker question randomly drawn from our question pool and your answer will be recorded. After the recording is done, you will go through a series of display screens asking for your evaluations about various music snippets. The session should take no more than 20 minutes. There are no right or wrong responses in the study. Your honest personal judgments are valuable to us.
